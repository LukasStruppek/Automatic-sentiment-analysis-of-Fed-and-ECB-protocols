[' Total nominal retail sales rose considerably in November, more than offsetting a drop in October; over the two months, retail sales were advancing at a slower pace than the average rate in the second and third quarters. Much of the November increase reflected strong gains in sales of consumer durables, including improved sales of motor vehicles. In the nondurables sector, a sizable rise in November about reversed a decline in October. Recent surveys of consumer sentiment pointed to generally positive attitudes. ']
