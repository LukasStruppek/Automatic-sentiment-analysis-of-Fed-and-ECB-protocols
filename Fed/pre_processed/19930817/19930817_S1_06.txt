[' Industrial production recovered in July from small declines in May and June. Manufacturing output rose in spite of a sizable cutback in motor vehicle assemblies: utility production registered a strong weather-related gain; and mining output declined further. Within manufacturing, the production of consumer durable goods other than automobiles and trucks rebounded in July, and the output of business equipment advanced further. Total utilization of industrial capacity edged higher in July, reflecting a substantial gain at electric utilities: utilization of manufacturing capacity was unchanged. ']
