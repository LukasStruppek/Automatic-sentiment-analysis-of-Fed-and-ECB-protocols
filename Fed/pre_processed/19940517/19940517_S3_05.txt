[' Residential construction was indicated to be robust across much of the country, with activity picking up rapidly in the aftermath of the unusually severe winter. The affordability of housing remained high by historical standards, and the appeal of home ownership had been enhanced somewhat by the apparent firming of house prices over the past year. In these circumstances, housing activity had been well sustained, although there were some indications that the higher mortgage rates now prevailing had begun to damp residential investment. ']
