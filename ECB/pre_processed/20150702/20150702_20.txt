Moving on to monetary and financial conditions, the EONIA forward curve had steepened since mid-April, notably at medium to longer horizons. Sovereign bond yield spreads had increased somewhat between April and May, while corporate spreads had broadly stabilised. Against the background of sharp increases in longer-term nominal yields in the euro area, real rates had likewise increased amid broadly stable inflation expectations. Nonetheless, financing conditions for corporates and households had, overall, improved somewhat further.
