Turning to prices, there were signs that the downward trend in euro area headline inflation had recently come to a halt. According to Eurostat’s flash estimate, euro area annual HICP inflation had recovered in March to ‑0.1%, from -0.3% in February and -0.6% in January. HICP inflation excluding energy and food had stood at 0.6% in March, after 0.7% in February and 0.6% in January.
