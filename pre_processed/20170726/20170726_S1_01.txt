 The manager of the System Open Market Account (SOMA) reported on developments in domestic and foreign financial markets over the period since the June FOMC meeting The intermeeting period was relatively uneventful Bond yields in advanced economies increased moderately, in part reflecting evolving market perceptions of prospects for foreign monetary policies US bond yields rose to a smaller degree, and the value of the dollar on foreign exchange markets decreased Implied volatility in fixed-income markets remained low Equity prices rose further, with notable advances in indexes for emerging markets
