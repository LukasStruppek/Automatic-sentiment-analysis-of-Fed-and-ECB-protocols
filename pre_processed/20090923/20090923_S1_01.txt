 The Manager of the System Open Market Account reported on recent developments in domestic and foreign financial markets The Manager also reported on System open market operations in Treasury securities, agency debt, and agency mortgage-backed securities (MBS) since the Committee's August 11-12 meeting By unanimous vote, the Committee ratified those transactions There were no open market operations in foreign currencies for the System's account during the intermeeting period Since the Committee met in August, the Federal Reserve's total assets had risen about $125 billion, on balance, to approximately $21 trillion, as the System's purchases of securities exceeded a further decline in usage of the System's credit and liquidity facilities
